Help and support

How do I access my appointments?

You can view and access your upcoming appointments here where you can click on an individual appointment to view their location and directions. appointments can also be accessed using the menu in the top right-hand corner of the screen.

Where do I check in?

You can check in here or from the menu in the top right-hand corner of the screen. If you have been asked for a QR code, you can find it here or from the home page, which you can also access using the menu in the top right-hand corner of the screen.

What do I do if I'm late?

If you are late, you will be prompted to provide this information when completing the check in process. If you are 5-10 minutes late, your appointment will still go ahead, however if you are more than 10 mimutes late your appointment will be cancelled automatically and must be rescheduled. If you would like to reschedule an appointment, you can do so here.
